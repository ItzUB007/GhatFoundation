The Ghat Foundation

The Ghat Foundation is a non-profit company named after the integral 'ghats' that serve as lifelines connecting the people of the Gangetic Belt with the holy river Ganga.
Ghats - the steps leading down to the river- are the nerve centers of cultural, religious, and economic life along the Ganges, hosting everything from daily rituals and grand festivals to local trade and livelihood activities.
Mirroring the role of these ghats, the Ghat Foundation endeavors to establish a connective, resilient, and harmonious relationship between the river, its surrounding environment, and the communities that rely on them.

We Strive To..

01 Bolster community resilience
02 Facilitate sustainable andrespectful livelihoods
03 Rejuvenate andpreserve the unique art, culture, and traditions of the Gangetic Belt.

Through a blend of environmental conservation, socioeconomic development, educational outreach, and cultural promotion, we strive to embody the spirit of the ghats as connectors — bridging the past and the future, humans and nature, tradition and innovation.

Our Aim..

Underlying our endeavors is the principle of environmental justice. We believe in the equitable distribution of environmental benefits, the fair allocation of environmental burdens, and the inclusiveparticipation of all community members in environmental decision-making. In this way, we hope to mirror theghats' inclusive spirit.
Through these efforts, the Ghat Foundation aims to cultivate a symbiotic relationship between the river anditspeople — a relationship that sustains andenriches life along the Ganges, just as the ghats havedone for millennia.
Like these timeless riverside platforms, we strive to be a place of connection, community, and mutual care, supporting the Ganges and itspeople today, tomorrow, and for generations to come.
